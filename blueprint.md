# PROKOLPO BLUEPRINT: RAJBARI PEPE CHASH (2026)
**Document ID:** RG-PAPAYA-001
**Last Updated:** 16 Feb, 2026
**Status:** Land Preparation Phase

## 1. SADHARON TOTHO (Basic Information)
- **Location:** Rajbari District, Bangladesh (Mati: Doash / Bele-Doash).
- **Total Area:** 15 Shotangsho (Decimal) / Pray 6,534 Sq. Ft.
- **Jat (Variety):** Red Lady (Taiwan 786) Hybrid - F1.
- **Ropon Somoy:** March er 1st Week, 2026.
- **Gach Sonkha:** 140-150 ti.
- **Durotto:** 6 ft (Row) x 6 ft (Gach).
- **Mul Uddeshya:** Sobji Pepe & Paka Pepe.

## 2. JOMI TOIRI BLUEPRINT (Land Preparation)
- **Podhoti:** "Bed & Drain System" (Rajbari te heavy brishti hoy tai).
- **Drainage:** Jomir charpashe o row-er majhe 1.5 ft govir drain/nala.
- **Mati Shodhon (pH):** Dolomite (Chun) - 15-20 KG (1st chash er somoy).
- **Mada Size (Pit):** 1.5 ft x 1.5 ft x 1.5 ft.
- **Bed Height:** Mati theke 6-8 inch uchu (Pyramid shape).

## 2.1 SATHI FOSHOL STRATEGY (Intercropping)
- **Approved Crops:** Lal Shak (Red Amaranth), Palong Shak (Spinach), Dhoniya Pata (Coriander).
- **NISIDDHO CROPS:** Tomato, Begun, Alu, Morich (Eki virus family - HIGH RISK).
- **Method:** Row-er majhkhaner faka jaygay (alleys) bij bunben prothom 45-60 diner jonno.
- **Benefit:** Sar abong nirani (weeding) khoroch uthe ashbe.

## 3. SAR BABOSTHAPONA (Nutrient Management)
### A. Basal Dose (Mada Toiri - Per Pit/Gorto)
Planting er 10-12 din age matir sathe mishaben:
- **Pocha Gobor:** 8-10 KG
- **TSP:** 150-200 gm
- **Gypsum:** 100 gm
- **Zinc Monohydrate:** 10 gm
- **Boron (Solubor):** 10 gm
- **Magnesium Sulphate:** 10-15 gm (Pata holud howa rodh kore)
- **Carbofuran (Furadan 5G):** 5-10 gm (Matir poka/nematode marar jonno)

### B. Prothom Top Dressing (25-30 Din Por)
Gacher gora theke 6-8 inch dure ring kore diben:
- **Urea:** 30 gm
- **MoP (Potash):** 30 gm
- **Sorishar Khoil:** 100 gm (3 din vijano) - Optional tobe dile valo.

### C. Ditiyo Top Dressing (60 Din Por / Ful ashar age)
- **Urea:** 50 gm
- **MoP (Potash):** 50 gm
- **DAP:** 50 gm

## 4. ROG-BALAI DAMAN (Crop Protection)
| Threat (Rog) | Prevention/Cure Strategy | Koto din por por |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mosaic Virus / Leaf Curl** | Vector Control (Sada Machi/Jab Poka): Spray Imidacloprid (Tido/Admire) @ 0.5ml/L OR Acetamiprid.<br>**Strict Weeding:** Gacher gora theke 4ft area te kono ghash rakha jabe na. | Proti 10-12 Din |
| **Gora Pocha (Root Rot)** | Fungicide: Mancozeb (Dithane M-45) @ 2gm/L + Carbendazim. Nala clear rakhun. | Borshakale / Monthly |
| **Makor (Mites)** | Miticide: Abamectin / Vertimec @ 1.2ml/L. | Jodi pata nicher dike kukuraya jay |
| **Boron Deficiency** | Micro-nutrient: Solubor Boron Spray @ 2gm/L. | Ful o fol ashar somoy |
