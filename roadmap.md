# PROJECT: RAJBARI GREEN GOLD (Pepe Chash Blueprint)

- **Jomi:** 15 Shotangsho (Decimal)
- **Location:** Rajbari District
- **Target:** 4-5 Mash e Profit (Sobji Pepe) & 7-9 Mash e Paka Pepe
- **Gach Sonkha:** 140-150 ti (Red Lady/Top Lady Hybrid)
- **Distance:** 6 ft x 6 ft

## 🗓️ PHASE 1: JOMI TOIRI O MADA PROSTUTI (Day 1 - Day 15)
*Ei dhapti sobcheye gurutto-purno. Vittti majboot hole gach shokto hobe.*

### Day 1: Prothom Chash O Agacha Porishkar
- [ ] Jomir joto agacha, jongol ache sob kete puriye felun.
- [ ] Power Tiller/Tractor diye 1ti Govir Chash din.
- [ ] Mati boro boro dhela (chunks) thakbe. Moi (Ladder) diben NA.
- [ ] 2 din rodh khawate din jate matir nicher poka o jibanu more jay.

### Day 3: Chun Proyog (Soil Treatment)
- [ ] **Material:** 15-20 KG Dolomite ba Pathure Chun.
- [ ] Puru jomite chun citiye din.
- [ ] Chun deyar por 4 din jomi fele rakhun. (Brishti hole valo, nahole halka pani citan).

### Day 7: Ditiyo Chash & Leveling
- [ ] **Material:** 600-700 KG Pocha Gobor Sar (ba 1 Tractor).
- [ ] Gobor sar jomite citiye din.
- [ ] 2nd Chash din (Ara-ari vabe).
- [ ] Moi (Ladder) diye jomi ekdom SOMAN (Level) korun. Kothao jeno gorto na thake.
- [ ] Jomir charpashe 1.5 ft govir Drain/Nala katun. Panir line clear rakhun.

### Day 8-10: Mada (Gorto) Toiri & Marking
- [ ] Fita diye mepe 6 ft por por khuti (Stick) putun. (Row to Row 6ft, Plant to Plant 6ft).
- [ ] **Gorto Size:** 1.5 ft (Length) x 1.5 ft (Width) x 1.5 ft (Depth).
- [ ] Gorter Upper Soil (Oporer mati) ek pashe rakhun, Lower Soil (Nicher mati) onno pashe.
- [ ] 2 din gorto gulo rode shukan.

### Day 12: Mada Vorat (Basal Dose)
*Nicher sar gulo shudu "Upper Soil" ba oporer matir sathe mishaben.*
**Proti Gorte Sarrer Poriman:**
- **Pocha Gobor:** 8-10 KG
- **TSP:** 150-200 Gram (Shikor baray)
- **Gypsum:** 100 Gram
- **Zinc:** 10 Gram
- **Boron:** 10 Gram (Fol aka-baka rodh kore)
- **Furadan/Carbofuran:** 5-10 Gram (Matir poka mare - MUST)
- **Magnesium (Jodi pan):** 10 Gram

- [ ] Sar mishano mati diye gorto vorat korun.
- [ ] Gorto vorat kore matir 10-12 inch uchu Dhibi (Pyramid) banan.
- [ ] Halka pani diye dhibi bhijiye din jate sar poche jay.
- [ ] **WARNING:** Ekhon Potash (MoP) diben na. Chara laganor por dibo.

### Day 15-18: Sathi Foshol Bunon (Intercropping)
- [ ] **Crop:** Lal Shak / Palong Shak (No Tomato/Brinjal!).
- [ ] **Method:** Dhibi/Mada baad diye, majhkhaner faka jaygay (alleys) bij citiye din.
- [ ] **Benefit:** Agacha kom hobe, extra income hobe.

## 🌱 PHASE 2: CHARA ROPON (Day 20 - Day 25)

### Day 20: Chara Songroho
- [ ] **Variety:** Red Lady (Taiwan 786) High Yielding.
- [ ] **Age:** 35-40 diner sustho chara kinun ba ready rakhun.
- [ ] Chara ene chayay (shade) 1-2 din rest din (Hardening).
- [ ] **Chara Shodhon (Crucial):** Roponer age polybag-er mati shoho chara ti Fungicide mixed panite (Autostin/Carbendazim) 10-15 minute dubiye rakhun. Etate *Root Rot* hoy na.

### Day 22-25: Ropon (Planting)
- [ ] **Time:** Poronto Bikel (4 PM er por).
- [ ] Dhibir mathay choto gorto korun (polythene bag er map onujayi).
- [ ] Blade diye charar polybag ti sabdhane ketun (Shikor jeno na vange).
- [ ] Chara bosiye mati diye chape din.
- [ ] **LEVEL:** Charar gorar mati jeno mul jomi theke 6-8 inch uchu thake.
- [ ] Sathe sathe Basher Khuti (Stick) gete gach bendhe din.
- [ ] Halka pani din.

## 🚀 PHASE 3: PORICHORJA O BARONTI (Day 26 - Day 60)

### Day 30: 1st Spray (Virus Protection)
*Rajbari te virus er attack hoy, tai agei protection nin.*
- [ ] **Spray:** Imidacloprid group (Jemon: Admire/Tido/Imitaf) - 1ml/Litre pani.
- [ ] **Fungicide:** Mancozeb (Dithane M-45) - 2gm/Litre pani.
- [ ] 10-15 din por por spray korun.
- [ ] **Irrigation Check (Hath Porikkha):** Matir niche theke ek mutho mati nin. Tip dile jodi 'dhela' hoy kintu chap dile venge jay = PERFECT. Pani porle = WET. Jhurjhure hole = DRY.

### Day 45: 1st Top Dressing (Opori Sar)
*Gach laganor 20-25 din por.*
- [ ] Gacher gora theke 6-8 inch dure ring (naala) kore sar din.
- [ ] **Urea:** 25-30 Gram
- [ ] **Potash (MoP):** 20-25 Gram
- [ ] Sar diye mati dhake din o halka seich din.

**Agacha Domon (Virus Control):**
- [ ] **Strict Rule:** Gacher gora theke 4 ft elakay KONO Ghash rakha jabe na.
- [ ] Jomi sob somoy porishkar rakhben. Agacha = Virus er basa (Whitefly thake).

## 🌸 PHASE 4: FLOWERING & FRUITING (Day 61 - Day 100)

### Day 65-70: Ful Dekha Dibe
- [ ] Red Lady te pray sob gachei ful asbe.
- [ ] Jodi purush gach (shudu lamba ful er chorra) dekhen, kete felun (Highest 5% rakhte paren).
- [ ] **2nd Top Dressing:**
    - **Urea:** 50 Gram
    - **Potash:** 50 Gram
    - Gacher gora theke 1 ft dure ring kore din.

### Day 80: Micronutrient Spray
- [ ] Foler size boro korar jonno "Solubor Boron" o "Chelated Zinc" spray kora valo.
- [ ] **Thining:** Ekta botaay jodi 3-4 ta pepe dhore, choto/beka gulo fele din. Ekta botaay 1-2 ta healthy pepe rakhun.

## 💰 PHASE 5: HARVESTING & PROFIT (Day 110 onwards)

### Day 110-120: Sobji Pepe Songroho
- [ ] Gach proti 4-5 ta kore boro "Green Papaya" tulun.
- [ ] Local bazar ba wholesale e bikri shuru korun.
- [ ] Etei sar o labor khoroch uthe jabe.

### Day 150+: Paka Pepe
- [ ] Pepe te holud rong dhorle (Color Break) songroho korun.
- [ ] Kagoj/Paper diye muriye box korun transport er jonno.

## ⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT (Rajbari Special)

**Jolaboddhota (Water logging):**
- **Solution:** Bed method (Mada uchu rakha).
- **Action:** Bhari brishti holei kodal niye nala clear korben. Gacher goray 24 ghonta pani jamlei gach morbe.

**Jhor/Batas:**
- **Solution:** Protiti gache shokto basher khuti din.
- **Action:** Gacher kanda (Stem) kokhonoi khutir sathe kosh-e badhben na, '8' shape e dhila kore badhben.

**Virus (Pata Kukrano):**
- **Solution:** Sada machi (White fly) marte hobe.
- **Action:** Jomi porishkar rakhun. Attack hole oi gach tiule maটির niche puthe felun, maya korben na.
